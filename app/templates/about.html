Policies

-Reward Points

  • Users earn reward points on reservations at a rate of 5 points per night on a reservation
  • A User can earn a maximum of 100 points
  • At a 100 points, users are able to redeem their points for a free night on their next reservation

    • -Cancellations are refunds

  • Cancellations are allowed up before the check in date of a reservation
  • Cancellations made after the check in date will not be refunded
  • While a user will be refunded for their cancellation, a 50$ cancellation fee will be applied
